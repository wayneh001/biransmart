Technique

Perfect communication through high speed and stable connection.

Through wire
KNX

The bus device in KNX can be a sensor or an actuator, and all functions can be controlled, monitored, and sent through a unified system, without the need for a control center.

RS485

RS485 uses the topological structure, master-slave communication mode, the central master controls the signal transmission between devices. RS485 adopts differential signal, which has a strong anti-interference ability and accurate timing positioning.

Biran adopts RS485 technology, which is cost-effective and has a high degree of compatibility for equipment expansion.

Through wire
Z-wave

Z-wave emerges short-range wireless communication technology based on radio frequency, low cost, low power consumption, high reliability, suitable for the network.

EnOcean

EnOcean is ultra-low power short-distance wireless communication technology based on energy harvesting is applied to indoor energy harvesting.

ZigBee

ZigBee is low-speed and short-distance transmission of wireless network protocols, low power consumption, cost, complexity, a large number of nodes, multiple network topologies, fast, reliable, and safe.

Biran adopts ZigBee 3.0, based on the IEEE802.15.4 standard, working frequency of 2.4GHz, using ZigBee-PRO network, unified from the leading wireless standards of the ZigBee Alliance. It is the first unified, open and complete wireless IoT product development solution.
