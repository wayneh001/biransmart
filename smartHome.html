Smart home

Integrated the facilities to build an efficient house.

What is a smart home?

To establish a home management system through integrated wiring, network communication, security, automatic control, audio, and video technology, etc., to improve home safety, convenience, and comfort, and to achieve an environmentally friendly and energy-saving living environment.

The smart home is an integrated industry that includes home decoration, Internet, digital product retail, medical care, and development platform.

The industry has both the characteristics of long-term home decoration and the rapid replacement of digital products.
